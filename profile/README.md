## Sustainable Fisheries Wales
This Github organization contains repositories for members and collaborators of the fisheries group at Bangor University's School of Ocean Sciences, conducting research and advisory work that supports the management of fish and shellfish stocks in Welsh waters. 

Our primary target species are:
- [King scallop (*Pecten maximus*)](http://sustainable-fisheries-wales.bangor.ac.uk/scallops.php.en#tab2)
- [Queen scallop (*Aequipecten opercularis*)](http://sustainable-fisheries-wales.bangor.ac.uk/scallops.php.en#tab2)
- [Edible Crab (*Cancer pagurus*)](http://sustainable-fisheries-wales.bangor.ac.uk/crabs.php.en#tab2)
- [European lobster (*Homarus gammarus*)](http://sustainable-fisheries-wales.bangor.ac.uk/lobsters.php.en#tab2)
- Common whelk (*Buccinum undatum*)
